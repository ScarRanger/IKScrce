About Us

Welcome to the "Indian Contributions in Astronomy" website! This project is a collaborative effort driven by a shared passion for the rich history of astronomical discoveries and advancements made in India. We aim to showcase the significant contributions of Indian astronomers and their lasting impact on the world's understanding of the cosmos.
