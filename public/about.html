About Us

India has a rich and ancient history of astronomical discoveries that have shaped the world’s understanding of the cosmos. From early scholars like Aryabhata and Bhaskaracharya who proposed heliocentric theories and calculated planetary movements, to marvels like Jantar Mantar used for observing celestial bodies, Indian astronomy has always blended science, mathematics, and spiritual insight.

This website is a tribute to those timeless contributions—providing basic information, images, interactive quizzes, and learning resources to help users appreciate India’s legacy in astronomy. Whether you're a student, teacher, or space enthusiast, our goal is to educate and inspire curiosity about how India looked to the stars—then and now.
