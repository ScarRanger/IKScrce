Introduction

India has made remarkable contributions to astronomy since ancient times, with notable advancements in celestial calculations, planetary motion, and astronomical instruments.
The Vedic texts, Puranas, and later works like the Surya Siddhanta laid the foundation for Indian astronomy.

The Scholars such as Aryabhata, Brahmagupta, and Bhaskara made significant contributions to our understanding of the cosmos, influencing global astronomical knowledge.
